Product Information - IM6930

Product Overview

Model: IM6930 - PLC Fundamentals Trainer
Product Range: Industrial Maintenance
Curriculum Codes: CP2388
Power Supply: 24V DC

Core Components

Siemens S7-1214C PLC, Unified Basic HMI (MTP700), DC motors with RPM feedback, proximity sensor, temperature transmitter, push buttons, selector switch, potentiometer, and emergency stop system.

Educational Focus

Designed for 16-20 year old engineering students and apprentices in further education, aligned with UK T-Level & BTEC qualifications in Maintenance, Installation & Repair.

Hands-on Training

12 guided worksheets covering PLC I/O basics, emergency stops, contact types, digital vs analogue I/O, output devices, and power supply components.

Fault Simulation

Hands-on testing with physical components, signal tracing, multimeter verification, and simulated dual-channel emergency stop system for educational purposes.

Safety & Standards

24V DC operation, touch-safe components, MCB protection, DIN rail mounting, and compliance with educational equipment standards.

About Halifax

Halifax is a historic market town in West Yorkshire, England, known for its rich industrial heritage and vibrant community. Located in the heart of Yorkshire, Halifax has been a center of commerce and industry for centuries.

Location & Heritage

Halifax is situated in the Calder Valley, surrounded by the beautiful Pennine hills. The town has a proud history of textile manufacturing, engineering, and innovation, making it an ideal location for educational technology companies like Matrix TSL.

Modern Halifax

Today, Halifax continues to be a thriving town with a mix of traditional industries and modern businesses. It serves as an important regional center for education, commerce, and culture in West Yorkshire.
